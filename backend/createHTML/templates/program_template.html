Solar Protocol Program Announcements

Spring, 2023

Sun Thinking

We are please to announce the first online exhibition, Sun Thinking, to be launched on the Solar Protocol Network in Spring of 2023.

Exhibition Launch: April 22nd, 12pm-2pm EST.
Format: Hybrid. Event held in person in New York City and online. Register via eventbrite.

Sun Thinking is an online exhibition that brings together artists and writers to explore some of the themes and questions central to the Solar Protocol Project. The exhibition includes some new commissions, as well as experimental projects and texts that critically and playfully explore the qualities and characteristics of solar-power as medium, the materiality of computation, life within planetary limits and environmental perspectives on intelligence.
