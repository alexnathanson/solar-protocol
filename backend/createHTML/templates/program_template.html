Solar Protocol Program Announcements

Spring, 2023

Thinking with the Sun

Launch: April 22nd, 12pm-2pm EST. Hybrid event (in person in New York City and online). Register here.

We are please to announce the first online exhibition, Thinking with the Sun, to be launched on the Solar Protocol Network in Spring of 2023.

Thinking with the Sun brings together artists and writers to explore some of the themes and questions that are central to the Solar Protocol Project. The exhibition includes some new commissions, as well as experimental projects and texts that critically and playfully explore the qualities and characteristics of solar-power as medium, the materiality of computation, life within planetary limits and environmental perspectives on intelligence.
