DASH begins with viral dashcam footage of the collision between a taxi and a Ferrari on the streets of Singapore. In an accompanying lecture, Ho treats the contemporary phenomenon of rewatching crash footage online as an entry point for understanding the broader logic of foresight. Repurposing film clips from Star Trek, an oil documentary by Shell, and others, Ho narrates the development of Singapore's Risk Assessment and Horizon Scanning Programme Office (RAHS). Situating Singapore as a critical node in global financial circuits and regional labour routes, Ho shifts attention from technosolutionist approaches to modelling crises toward the politics of anticipatory frameworks.
